Watch less. Learn more.

10xTube adds fast text and audio summaries directly inside YouTube, so long videos become compact takeaways you can scan or listen to.

How to Listen Feedback & Suggestions

Features

Text summaries

Open readable YouTube summaries from video cards, search results, subscriptions, watch pages, and other YouTube surfaces.

Timestamp links

Use linked timestamps to jump to the right moment in the current video without closing the summary.

Spoken summaries

Click Listen and the summary is read aloud with your browser's built-in voice — free, out of the box. Optional Azure voices are there if you want audiobook quality. How Listen works.

How It Works

10xTube runs locally in your browser. It uses YouTube's standard Ask summary response from your browser session and adds page-aware summary panels on YouTube.

Reading, listening with the built-in voice, and timestamp seeking all work without any account or key. Azure Speech is an optional upgrade for nicer voices, using your own key.
